Conclusion

After reading all of these reasons, watching the video, and looking at the graph, it is clear to see that the Dodgers should be your clear pick for the best organization in baseball and the best team in the league. Based off of the success in the past and the recent additions that will lead the team in years to come, the Dodgers are prone to continue to have great years and be annual contenders to win the World Series every year. They have only continued to get stronger and stronger and given what has been done in the past, they will not shy away from signing or trading for even more star players. In my opinion, they are also the most fun team to watch with the best fanbase. Los Angeles is already a pretty cool city, and the Dodgers being in LA only add to it.
